INFO

Fabric's Bullet Physics integration allows to create large scale physics simulations and run them at interactive framerates. In this sample we create a chain of rigidbodies by connecting all of the spheres with ball-socket constraints (called Point2Point constraint in bullet). On GPUs supporting OpenGL 3.2+ hardware based instancing will be used.
You can click and drag on the rigidbodies to introduce a force to the simulation.
